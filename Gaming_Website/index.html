Brief Description

Final Fantasy 9 is a fantasy jrpg turn based game published by Square where you play as the thief Zidane with a plot surrounding Garnet the princess of Alexandria and tons of fascinating political intrigue at play. It is the 9th game in the series franchise and one of the very best there is. You'll meet memorable characters along the way who will join your party and travel through a variety of interesting landscapes while engaging in combat with enemies.
